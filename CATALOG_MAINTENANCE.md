# Catalog Maintenance

- When adding new games, insert them at the top of the catalog, before the existing entries.
- Keep data/games.json and data/games.js in the same order.
- When choosing a gameplay video, require the exact game title in the YouTube video title. Prioritize candidates in this order: exact title match, gameplay/walkthrough content, longer duration, then PS4/PS5/PlayStation mention.
- Use `video-selection.js` as the reference scoring logic for future video updates.
